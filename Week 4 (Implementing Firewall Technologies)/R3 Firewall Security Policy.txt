R3 Firewall Security Policy
---------------------------

Internal Network (Trusted):
- Can initiate any type of traffic (TCP, UDP, ICMP) to the Internet.
- Can receive return traffic from the Internet (responses to internal requests).
- Cannot initiate or receive any traffic to/from the Conference Room Network.
- No unsolicited traffic from the Internet is allowed.

Conference Room Network (Untrusted):
- Can initiate only web traffic (HTTP - port 80, HTTPS - port 443) to the Internet.
- Can receive return web traffic from the Internet (responses to HTTP/HTTPS requests).
- Cannot initiate or receive any traffic to/from the Internal Network.
- All other types of traffic are blocked.

Internet:
- Cannot initiate any traffic to the Internal or Conference Room Networks.
- Can only respond to traffic that was initiated from within the R3 site (either Internal or Conference Room networks).

Summary:
- No direct communication is allowed between the Internal and Conference Room networks.
- Only return traffic is allowed from the Internet.
- Only the Internal network has full access to the Internet.
- The Conference Room network is restricted to web browsing only.
